Delete Your BridgR Account

If you would like to request deletion of your BridgR account and associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to bridgrplatform@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My BridgR Account".
  2. Include the email address you used to create your account.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your owner account details (email, profile).
  • Library data managed under your account (students, seats, fees, etc.).

Data That May Be Retained

  • Financial/transaction records for up to 6 months for compliance purposes.
  • Aggregated or anonymized usage data that cannot identify you personally.

Once your request is verified, we will process deletion within 30 days and notify you by email.
